Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 20, 2026

BNM Exchange Rates ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

The App does not collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Files
  • Financial information
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device identifiers

Exchange Rate Data

The App retrieves publicly available exchange rate information published by the National Bank of Moldova (BNM).

The information is used only to display official exchange rates and historical data.

Internet Connection

Internet access is used only for downloading official exchange rate information and related public metadata.

No personal information is transmitted.

Offline Storage

Exchange rate data is stored locally on your device to provide offline access.

This data never leaves your device.

Third-party Services

The App does not use:

  • Advertising SDKs
  • Google Analytics
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Facebook SDK
  • Tracking technologies

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Sharing

No user information is sold, shared or transferred to third parties.

Security

Communication with official exchange rate services uses secure HTTPS connections whenever supported.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any future changes will be published on this page.
